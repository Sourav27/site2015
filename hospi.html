The Hospitality team at Mechanica will be at your service to cater to all your doubts throughout Mechanica. Registrations for a few events will also be done at the Hospitality Desk. For event registration at the Hospitality Desk, production of the Mechanica ID is mandatory. Also, you can approach the hospitality desk for other information like updates, event results, daily events, etc. The Hospitality Desk will be functional throughout the day from 9 A.M. to 5 P.M.
